# RACE Framework

## Czym jest?

RACE Framework to model customer journey dla digital marketingu, stworzony przez Dr. Dave'a Chaffeya (Smart Insights, 2010), opisujący cztery kluczowe etapy online marketing funnel: **Reach, Act, Convert, Engage**. Framework powstał jako praktyczna, actionable alternatywa dla tradycyjnych funnel models (AIDA, Purchase Funnel), specyficznie designed dla digital/omnichannel environment.

RACE integruje całą customer journey od pierwszego kontaktu (awareness) przez transakcję aż po long-term relationship, jednocześnie mapując konkretne digital tactics i KPIs dla każdego stage. To nie tylko model teoretyczny, ale operational framework do planowania, wykonania i pomiaru digital marketing campaigns.

## Dla kogo jest?

- **Digital Marketerów** - planujących integrated campaigns across channels
- **Marketing Managerów** - zarządzających full-funnel strategy
- **E-commerce teams** - optymalizujących conversion i retention
- **Content Marketerów** - mapujących content do customer journey stages
- **Agencje marketingowe** - strukturyzujących client campaigns

## Co można z tego uzyskać?

1. **Full-funnel visibility** - holistyczny widok od awareness do advocacy
2. **Aligned KPIs** - clear metrics dla każdego stage
3. **Tactical clarity** - konkretne actions i channels dla każdego etapu
4. **Balanced investment** - alokacja budżetu across całej journey, nie tylko acquisition
5. **Customer-centric planning** - myślenie od perspektywy customer journey nie channels

## Dlaczego warto z tego skorzystać?

- **Digital-first design** - stworzony dla online/omnichannel, nie adaptacja offline modelu
- **Actionable** - każdy stage ma clear tactics, tools, metrics
- **Simple ale kompletny** - 4 stages łatwe do zapamiętania, pokrywają full journey
- **Industry adoption** - używany przez digital marketing teams globally
- **Free resources** - Smart Insights oferuje templates, guides, benchmarks

## Cztery etapy RACE

### R - Reach (Dotarcie)

**Cel:** Build awareness i drive traffic - dotrzeć do potential customers w różnych digital channels.

**Customer mindset:** "Nie wiem jeszcze o Tobie" lub "Poszukuję rozwiązania"

**Key Question:** Jak zwiększyć brand visibility i attract więcej visitors?

#### Tactics i Channels:

**Paid Media (Push):**
- **Display ads:** Google Display Network, programmatic
- **Social ads:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok ads
- **Search ads (branded):** Google Ads, Bing Ads
- **Video ads:** YouTube, social video ads
- **Native advertising:** Sponsored content on publishers

**Owned Media:**
- **SEO:** Organic search visibility
- **Content marketing:** Blog posts, guides, videos ranking in search
- **Social media presence:** Organic posts, community building
- **Email (cold outreach):** Lead generation campaigns

**Earned Media:**
- **PR:** Press mentions, media coverage
- **Influencer marketing:** Partnerships, mentions
- **Social sharing:** Viral content, word-of-mouth
- **Reviews/ratings:** Third-party validation

#### Key Metrics (KPIs):

- **Impressions:** Ile razy content był seen
- **Reach:** Unique users exposed
- **Traffic:** Website visits, app opens
- **Share of Voice:** % of conversation in category
- **Brand awareness lift:** Pre/post campaign surveys
- **Cost per 1000 impressions (CPM)**
- **Click-through rate (CTR):** % impressions → clicks

#### Optimization Focus:

- **Audience targeting:** Prawidłowe segmenty, lookalikes
- **Message-market fit:** Ad creative resonating z audience
- **Channel mix:** Balance paid/owned/earned
- **Budget allocation:** ROI per channel

**Przykład:**
- **Dollar Shave Club viral video:** 27M views (Reach) → massive brand awareness → 12,000 orders w first 48h

### A - Act (Akcja/Interakcja)

**Cel:** Engage visitors - skłonić ich do interakcji z brand, explore value proposition.

**Customer mindset:** "Zainteresowany, chcę dowiedzieć się więcej"

**Key Question:** Jak engage visitors i move ich deeper do funnel?

#### Tactics:

**On-site engagement:**
- **Landing page optimization:** Clear value prop, compelling copy
- **Content experience:** Blog posts, videos, interactive tools
- **Lead magnets:** Ebooks, webinars, free tools w exchange za email
- **Product discovery:** Intuitive navigation, search, filters
- **Social proof:** Reviews, testimonials, case studies visible
- **Live chat/chatbots:** Immediate assistance

**Multi-touch engagement:**
- **Retargeting ads:** Following visitors across web
- **Email nurture sequences:** Drip campaigns educating prospects
- **Social engagement:** Responding, community building
- **Webinars/events:** Educational content

**Personalization:**
- **Dynamic content:** Content based on source, behavior, segment
- **Recommendations:** "You might like..."
- **Behavioral triggers:** Pop-ups based on actions (exit intent, scroll depth)

#### Key Metrics (KPIs):

- **Bounce rate:** % visitors leaving without interaction (lower = better)
- **Pages per session:** Depth of engagement
- **Time on site:** Duration of visit
- **Content engagement:** Video views, scroll depth, downloads
- **Lead generation rate:** % visitors → leads (email signups, demo requests)
- **Cost per lead (CPL)**
- **Social engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, follows

#### Optimization Focus:

- **User experience (UX):** Fast load times, mobile optimization, intuitive nav
- **Content relevance:** Right content dla right stage
- **Calls-to-action (CTAs):** Clear, compelling next steps
- **Trust signals:** Security badges, guarantees, testimonials

**Przykład:**
- **HubSpot:** Offering free tools (Website Grader, Email Signature Generator) w exchange za email → high Act conversion → nurture do paid product

### C - Convert (Konwersja)

**Cel:** Turn prospects into customers - drive transactions lub key conversions.

**Customer mindset:** "Gotowy kupić" lub "Evaluating options"

**Key Question:** Jak maximize conversion rate i revenue?

#### Tactics:

**Conversion optimization:**
- **Checkout optimization:** Simplified process, guest checkout, multiple payment options
- **Pricing strategy:** Tiered pricing, discounts, urgency (limited time offers)
- **Free trials/freemium:** Lower barrier to entry
- **Product demos:** Live lub self-serve
- **Comparison tools:** Help decision-making
- **Guarantees:** Money-back, free returns

**Persuasion elements:**
- **Scarcity/Urgency:** "Only 3 left", "Sale ends tonight"
- **Social proof:** "10,000 customers trust us"
- **Authority:** Certifications, awards, expert endorsements
- **Reciprocity:** Free shipping, bonus gifts

**Abandonment recovery:**
- **Cart abandonment emails:** Reminders z incentives
- **Retargeting ads:** Following cart abandoners
- **Exit-intent pop-ups:** Last-minute offers

#### Key Metrics (KPIs):

- **Conversion rate:** % visitors → customers
- **Average order value (AOV):** Revenue per transaction
- **Revenue:** Total sales
- **Cost per acquisition (CPA):** Marketing cost per customer
- **Return on ad spend (ROAS):** Revenue / Ad spend
- **Shopping cart abandonment rate:** % carts not completed
- **Lead-to-customer conversion rate:** % leads → paying (B2B)

#### Optimization Focus:

- **A/B testing:** Headlines, CTAs, pricing, layouts
- **Funnel analysis:** Gdzie drop-offs occur
- **Payment friction:** Simplify checkout, trust signals
- **Objection handling:** FAQs, live chat support

**Przykład:**
- **Amazon:** 1-Click ordering, Prime free shipping, "Frequently bought together" → optimized every aspect dla conversion

### E - Engage (Zaangażowanie/Retencja)

**Cel:** Build long-term relationships - retain customers, increase lifetime value, encourage advocacy.

**Customer mindset:** "Jestem klientem, jaką wartość dostarczasz ongoing?"

**Key Question:** Jak retain customers i turn them into advocates?

#### Tactics:

**Post-purchase engagement:**
- **Onboarding emails:** Welcome series, product education
- **Thank you campaigns:** Confirmation, delivery updates
- **Customer support:** Proactive help, knowledge bases, chat
- **Product education:** Tutorials, webinars, certification programs

**Retention programs:**
- **Loyalty/Rewards programs:** Points, tiers, VIP benefits
- **Email marketing:** Newsletters, product updates, personalized recommendations
- **Subscription models:** Recurring revenue, consistent engagement
- **Exclusive content:** Members-only access

**Win-back & expansion:**
- **Re-engagement campaigns:** Targeting inactive customers
- **Upsell/Cross-sell:** Relevant product recommendations
- **Replenishment reminders:** "Time to reorder?"

**Advocacy & referral:**
- **Referral programs:** "Give $10, Get $10"
- **User-generated content:** Customer reviews, photos, testimonials
- **Social advocacy:** Encourage sharing, tagging
- **NPS surveys:** "How likely are you to recommend?"

#### Key Metrics (KPIs):

- **Customer retention rate:** % customers who stay
- **Churn rate:** % customers who leave
- **Customer lifetime value (CLV/LTV):** Total revenue per customer over lifecycle
- **Repeat purchase rate:** % customers buying again
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Loyalty i advocacy measure
- **Engagement rate:** Email open rates, app usage frequency
- **Referral rate:** % customers referring others
- **Customer satisfaction (CSAT):** Post-purchase satisfaction

#### Optimization Focus:

- **Personalization at scale:** Segment-based communications
- **Value delivery:** Continue providing value post-purchase
- **Community building:** Forums, user groups, events
- **Feedback loops:** Listen i act na customer feedback

**Przykład:**
- **Spotify:** Personalized playlists (Discover Weekly, Release Radar), yearly Wrapped summary, social sharing features → high engagement i retention

## RACE jako cykl (nie linear funnel)

**Kluczowa koncepcja:** RACE nie jest linear - to **cycle**.

```
    REACH
      ↓
    ACT
      ↓
   CONVERT
      ↓
   ENGAGE
      ↓
    REACH (via advocacy, referrals, sharing)
```

**Engaged customers become Reach:**
- Social sharing → new Reach
- Referrals → new Reach
- Reviews → new Reach (SEO, social proof)
- Repeat purchases → ongoing cycle

## RACE + Planning: Integrating channels i content

### Mapping Tactics do RACE:

| Channel | Reach | Act | Convert | Engage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **SEO** | Rankings, organic traffic | On-site content engagement | Product pages optimize | Fresh content for return visits |
| **Social Media** | Brand awareness posts, ads | Interactive content, comments | Social commerce, promos | Community, customer stories |
| **Email** | Cold outreach (B2B) | Lead nurture sequences | Promo emails, cart abandonment | Newsletters, loyalty programs |
| **Paid Search** | Brand awareness | Drive to landing pages | PPC dla transactional keywords | Remarketing do existing customers |
| **Content Marketing** | Blog SEO | Gated content (ebooks) | Case studies, comparison guides | Ongoing education, webinars |

### Content mapping do RACE:

| Stage | Content Types | Intent |
|---|---|---|
| **Reach** | Blog posts, infographics, social posts, videos | Awareness, educational, entertaining |
| **Act** | Ebooks, guides, webinars, tools | Consideration, learning |
| **Convert** | Product pages, demos, case studies, pricing, trials | Evaluation, decision |
| **Engage** | Newsletters, tutorials, community content, updates | Retention, expansion, advocacy |

## RACE Benchmarks (przykładowe)

**E-commerce:**
- **Reach:** CPM $5-15, CTR 0.5-2%
- **Act:** Bounce rate 40-60%, Pages/session 3-5
- **Convert:** Conversion rate 1-3%, AOV varies widely
- **Engage:** Repeat purchase 20-30%, Email open 15-25%

**SaaS B2B:**
- **Reach:** CPM $10-50 (LinkedIn), CTR 1-3%
- **Act:** Lead gen conversion 2-5%
- **Convert:** Lead→Customer 10-20%, Sales cycle 3-12 months
- **Engage:** Churn <5% (annual), NPS 30-50

## RACE w praktyce: Campaign Planning

**Example: E-commerce Fashion Brand - Holiday Campaign**

### Reach (November 1-15):
- **Channels:** Instagram/Facebook ads, Google Display, influencer partnerships
- **Content:** Lookbooks, gift guides, trend videos
- **Goal:** 5M impressions, 100K website visits
- **Budget:** $50K

### Act (November 15 - December 10):
- **Channels:** Retargeting ads, email nurture, social engagement
- **Content:** Styling tips, size guides, early-bird discounts
- **Goal:** 30% visitors → email list, 5 pages/session
- **Budget:** $30K

### Convert (December 1-24):
- **Channels:** Promo emails, PPC, cart abandonment
- **Content:** Flash sales, free shipping, gift wrapping
- **Goal:** 3% conversion rate, $500K revenue
- **Budget:** $70K

### Engage (December 25 - ongoing):
- **Channels:** Thank you emails, loyalty program, social
- **Content:** Style inspiration, care guides, exclusive previews
- **Goal:** 25% repeat purchase w Q1, NPS 40+
- **Budget:** $20K

## RACE vs inne frameworks

### RACE vs AIDA (Awareness, Interest, Desire, Action):
- **AIDA:** Classic, pre-digital, ends at purchase
- **RACE:** Includes post-purchase (Engage), digital-specific tactics

### RACE vs Purchase Funnel (Awareness → Consideration → Purchase):
- **Funnel:** Linear, transactional focus
- **RACE:** Cyclical, relationship focus

### RACE vs AARRR (Pirate Metrics):
- **AARRR:** Product-led growth, analytics focus
- **RACE:** Marketing-led, campaign planning focus
- **Overlap:** Both full-funnel, retention important
